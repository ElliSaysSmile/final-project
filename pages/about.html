About us:


Honey Badger Consignments was born in 2005 when owner Melani graduated highschool. She was never that interested in college but began working in her parent's thrift store at the age of 14. All of this time she maintained a passion for fashion as well maintaining her work ethic allowing her to have one of the most successful consignment stores in New York City.

Over the last 15 years, Melani has allowed the residents of New York to access the high end fashion brands they crave, without having to pay top dollar for them. If you have any further questions about our store or our intake process, please use our contact form!
