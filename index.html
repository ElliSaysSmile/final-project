Why Consign?

The world is becoming more and more conscious of how much we waste. There are many ways that we can positively impact our environment and clothing is not an exception. Instead of throwing out once loved pieces it would be a beautiful idea to give those pieces a new life in the hands of someone else who will love them! You can also make money in the process!

Easy as 1. 2. 3.

1. Drop off your items!
Please make sure the items are in good condition and current (purchased no more than 3 years ago!) Browse the store while you wait about 20 minutes for the intake staff to evaluate your items.


2. Wait!
Nobody likes to wait but the intake staff takes time to price and display your items in the store. This can take up to two weeks. Please also allow time for your items to sell. We recommend checking back no sooner than 1 month.



3. Get paid!
After you have waited please come into the store to check your balance or cashout! You can also check your balance by calling 888-888-8888
